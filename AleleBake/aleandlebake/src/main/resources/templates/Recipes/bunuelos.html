BUÑUELOS DE VIENTO


December 12,2019

Celebrate Christmas the Mexican way with these crispy churro-flavored buñuelos

Prep time: 30 min.


Portions: 20 pieces


Difficulty: Easy



INGREDIENTS

  • 175 gr. AP flour

  • 300 ml. of milk

  • A pinch of salt

  • A tsp of vanilla extract

  • 1 cup white sugar

  • 2 tsp of cinnamon

PROCEDURE

  1. Place in a blender the flour, milk, vanilla extract and salt.

  2. Place oil in two recipients and heat.

  3. Once oil is ready, in one recipient place the mold for about a minute.

  4. Pat the mold in a paper towel to remove excess oil, and immediately after, insert the mold in the flour mix, making sure that the mix doesn't cover the mold.

  5. Submerge the mold in the oil and shake it gently in order to release the buñuelo. If your mold is brand new, you might need to use the tip of a fork to push it out of the mold

  6. Once the buñuelo is golden place it in a paper towel to remove the excess oil

  7. Mix the sugar an cinnamon, and toss the buñuelos in this mix.

  8. Enjoy!

NOTE: If you want to keep your buñuelos fresh, place them in a tight cello bag once they are cold. This will keep them crispy for up to five days.
