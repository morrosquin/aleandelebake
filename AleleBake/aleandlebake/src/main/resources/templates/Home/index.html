ABOUT US

We are mother and son. I'm Ale, a self-taught baker, born and raised in Mexico, where I had the great fortune of spending my youth in my parents' farm. The sweet smell of the freshly baked apple bread that my mom made every morning at the farm is my most precious childhood memory.

When I became a mother, I wanted to recreate for Ele (pictured above), my three-year old son, that love-at-first-bite experience me and my siblings had when tasting my mother's baked goods. I just had a tiny problem: I didn't know how to bake.

My kitchen became the testing lab for many video, blog and print recipes. Most of my relatives and close friends were the guinea pigs for these awful oven experiments.

My first pastries were terrible, but little by little, I found a way to master the skill and combine ingredients and even integrate some Mexican and Latin American flavors in my creations. They were so tasty, even my picky son wanted to help me in the kitchen as a "flavor inspector".

That's how Ale&Ele Bake was born.
